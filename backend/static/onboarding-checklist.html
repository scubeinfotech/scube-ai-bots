Step 1 — Basic Setup
  • Business name & website URL
    We'll crawl your website daily to keep the AI updated with your latest content.
  • Services / Products list
    A list of what you offer (text, Word doc, or PDF). The AI will answer questions about these.
  • FAQs or knowledge base
    Optional but recommended. Upload any existing FAQ documents for more accurate answers.
  • Contact details to share with customers
    Address, phone, email, business hours. The AI will provide these when asked.
  • Welcome message
    First message users see when they open the chat. E.g. "Hi! I'm the Acme AI Assistant. How can I help?"
Step 2 — Web Chat Widget
  • Add snippet to your website
    Paste before closing </body> tag on every page.
  • Test on desktop & mobile
  • Confirm welcome message shows
  • Test 3–4 questions
    Verify AI answers correctly from your knowledge base.
<!-- Paste before </body> --> <script src="https://chat.scubeinfotech.com.sg/widget.js" data-tenant-id="YOUR_TENANT_ID" data-api-key="YOUR_API_KEY" data-color="#2563eb"> </script>
Step 3 — WhatsApp (Optional)
  • Dedicated WhatsApp Business number
    A phone number (SIM or virtual) only for this chatbot.
  • Meta Business Account
    Create at business.facebook.com if not already done.
  • Create WhatsApp App on Meta
    developers.facebook.com → Create App → WhatsApp Business.
  • Send us 3 values from Meta Console:
    ① Phone Number ID
    ② Business Account ID
    ③ System User Access Token
  • Set webhook in Meta Console
    URL & Verify Token — we'll provide these once configured.
  • Send test message to your number
    Verify the AI replies automatically.
What We Provide After Setup
  • Tenant ID
    Your unique identifier on the platform.
  • API Key
    For securing your chat widget.
  • Widget embed code
    Ready to paste into your website.
  • Admin dashboard access
    View conversations, leads, and analytics.
  • WhatsApp webhook URL & verify token
    For Meta Console configuration.
  • Daily auto-crawl of your website
    Knowledge base updates every night at 3 AM.
✅ Go-live time: Web chat can go live within 2 hours of receiving your details. WhatsApp typically within 24 hours after Meta credentials are shared.
⚠️ WhatsApp note: Meta requires your number to be verified and your app to be in Live mode before real customers can message you. Allow 1–3 business days for Meta approval.
