1. How to Add the Chatbot to Your Home Page

To integrate our AI-powered chatbot, simply add the following code snippet just before the closing </body> tag of your website’s home page:

<!-- AI Chatbot Widget (Sample) -->
<script src="https://your-domain.com/widget/chatbot.js"></script>

Note: The above code is a sample. Please ask our team to get your personalized integration code after sharing your website details, business information, email ID, a welcome message sample, and your core business idea. This ensures the chatbot is tailored for your needs.

Once integrated, the chatbot will appear in the bottom-right corner, ready to assist your visitors.
