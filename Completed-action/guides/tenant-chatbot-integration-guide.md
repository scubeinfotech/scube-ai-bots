# Add Our AI Chatbot to Your Website

## 1. How to Add the Chatbot to Your Home Page

To integrate our AI-powered chatbot, simply add the following code snippet just before the closing `</body>` tag of your website’s home page:

```html
<!-- AI Chatbot Widget -->
<script src="https://your-domain.com/widget/chatbot.js"></script>
```

That’s it! The chatbot will appear in the bottom-right corner, ready to assist your visitors.
